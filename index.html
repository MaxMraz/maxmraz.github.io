About Me:

Max Mraz is a successful artist and music producer leveraging his creative talents and passion into making well-designed software. He completed a full-time coding bootcamp focusing on hands-on projects, designing and developing professionally programmed applications.

In addition to coding school, he is creating Ocean's Heart, a 2D action-adventure game. This has given him the opportunity to learn about many other aspects of design, from UI to sound design, to experience leading a small team.

Virtual Pet

A tomagachi-like console program, wherein the user finds a small robot and must take care of it. Teach it about the world and try to get on its good side before it becomes self-aware and causes the singularity. Take your robot on adventures such as trips to the grocery store or the park. Plant flowers together. Teach your robot not to hack the FBI's mainframe.

Java, Object-Oriented Programming Principals, Text Processing, Main Loops

Virtual Pet Shelter

The logical continuation of the Virtual Pet project, in which you find yourself operating Max and Riley's Shelter for Abandoned and Feral Pet Rocks. Adopt and care for as many pet rocks as you want, but each will need to be fed, watered, and played with or else they may crumble to dust.

Java, Test-Driven Design and Unity Tests, Collections, Pair Programming

Ocean's Heart

A 2D action adventure game. Play as a daughter searching an elaborate and detailed archipelago for her father, who is searching the same world for the pirates that attacked their home. Fight through dungeons, help quirky characters, uncover mysteries in ancient ruins.

Lua Scripting, API use, Graphic Design, UX Design, Sound Design, Project Management, Marketing
