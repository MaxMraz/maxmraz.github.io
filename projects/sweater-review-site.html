Greg's Sweaters, Reviewed

A school project to make a review website. In this case, a website created to review the sweater collection of someone's friend. As you scroll through the sometimes-harsh reviews of an oft-mocked collection of sweaters, you realize the depts of the friendship between Greg and the writer.

HTML, Spring Boot, MVC, Dynamic Webpages and Templates, Mockito, Thymeleaf, Gradle
